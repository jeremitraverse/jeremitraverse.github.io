Rule of Five

Set of guidelines that deal with resource management and custom implementations of the special member functions. Destructor Copy Constructor Copy Assignment Operator Move Constructor Move Assignment Operator The Rule of Five states that if you explicitly declare or define any of the following special member functions, then you should explicitly declare or define all of the remaining four special member functions.
